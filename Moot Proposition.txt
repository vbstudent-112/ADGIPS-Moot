Indea is the world’s largest democracy, standing as a vibrant testament to Unity in Diversity. This South Asian giant is home to over 1.4 billion people, offering a rich tapestry of religions, languages, and cultural traditions that span millennia. From the majestic peaks of the Himalayas to its vast coastal plains, Indea’s geography is as varied as its history. Today, it is not only celebrated for its ancient heritage and architectural marvels but also recognized as a global hub for innovation, technology, and economic growth. 


Rachel Sharma, an independent woman who is 25 years old, resides in Dilli. She is pursuing her graduation in Bachelors in Arts from Dilli University. She works as a social media influencer and is passionate about her career, as she is known worldwide for her engaging content which keeps her in fame among her followers. She has over 4 million followers on Snapgram (a social media platform with worldwide connectivity). She has always claimed to be a good friend to her batchmates. She gains more attention on social media narrating daily incidents of her life by being live on snapgram with her followers, where one day on 17 April, 2024 she tells an incident of her batchmate Mike Rathore that how ugly he is and he was a failure at life also showing his videos in college’s abandoned pump house where Mike was being bullied by many people whereas the faces of people were unclear, but Mike’s face was displayed publicly. Rachel said she never talked to Mike but felt pity for him. The matter was thereby never visited again by Rachel, but her followers made funny memes on Mike displaying them publicly on Rachel’s fan page. Months passed and her fans got new stories to hear and eventually people forgot about Mike’s incident. Mike commented on a post of Rachel that he would take his revenge, which was deleted by Rachel same day itself.


On 25th June, 2025, she came across a deepfake video on snapgram portraying her inappropriately engaging in sexually offensive activity. Soon she started getting public hate and her public image was disrupted. She tried to publicly clarify that she was not engaged in any such activity and that all such videos are fake & fabricated. She also made a cyber complaint at Mohini Police Station, Dilli. The deepfake content available in Indea was
deliberately removed by the respective authorities within 2 months but it had already caused severe damage to her mental health and public image.


Investigation by the Mohini Police Station cyber cell revealed that the content was created using the Personality Model feature of DeepSearch, an AI bot from Shina. This feature allows users to clone any person’s digital identity using just 30 seconds of existing footage. Although, under pressure, Indean authorities managed to block the specific URLs in Indea within two months. However, the DeepSearch tool remains accessible via VPNs and mirrors, and the company has refused to disable the Racheal Personality Model from its global library, claiming that it is a neutral creative tool not bound by Indean laws. The company also withdrew from any liability from any malicious use of their AI programme citing their ‘terms of use’ that company is mere service provider and removing any user’s content is against their policy of uninterrupted services; thus diverging from their policies would question their customer credibility.


It was further alleged that the person behind the making of the video was Mike Rathore who also studies BA at Dilli University with her and resided alone in DU hostel. He was confronted, and it was revealed that he was bullied by Rachel and her friends during their college. Rachel and her friends used to torture Mike and forced him to do activities that no normal person would be comfortable doing them. Mike was so traumatized that he later dropped out of college without completing his degree which caused his career a huge downfall.


Rachel filed a Writ Petition under Article 226 in High Court of Dilli, seeking a Global Takedown Order against DeepSearch Inc. and a direction to the Union of Indea to ban the tool for the entire Nation. The High Court granted an injunction against the content but refused to pass orders against DeepSearch Inc., stating that the court lacked extraterritorial jurisdiction over a company with no servers or offices in Indea. It further held that Personality Rights are not explicitly codified in Indean statutes.
 
Aggrieved by the High Court's limited relief, Rachel filed a Special Leave Petition (SLP) under Article 136 of the Indean Constitution before the Hon’ble Supreme Court of Indea.


1.Whether the Indean Judiciary can exercise jurisdiction under Section 75 of the IT Act, 2000 and Section 1 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) over a foreign AI developer (DeepSearch Inc.) whose software is used to target and harm Indean citizens, despite having no physical presence in Indea ?
2.Whether a Generative AI tool like DeepSearch, which provides the specific technology to mimic a persona (the Personality Model), can claim Safe Harbour protection under Section 79 of the IT Act, or if it should be classified as a Content Creator/Publisher due to the synthetic nature of the output ?
3.Whether the Right to Digital Integrity and Personality Rights are inherent to the Right to Life and Liberty (Article 21), and if so, do they include a Right to be Forgotten that mandates the deletion of AI training data derived from a person’s likeness without consent ?
4.Whether the Union of Indea has failed in its constitutional duty to protect its citizens from Digital Assault by not implementing a robust regulatory framework for AI-generated deepfakes and whether Mr. Mike Rathore could be made subject to any criminal liability based upon his threat to take revenge from Ms. Rachel ?
