# Moot Court Repository

## 📌 Overview

This repository contains all research materials, drafts, and supporting documents prepared for participation in a moot court competition. It is intended to serve as a centralized workspace for organizing legal research, memorial drafting, and case preparation.

---

## ⚖️ Contents

The repository may include (but is not limited to):

* **Memorials**

  * Petitioner Memorial
  * Respondent Memorial

* **Research Materials**

  * Case law compilations
  * Statutory provisions
  * Articles and journal references
  * Policy papers and reports

* **Arguments**

  * Issue-wise argument drafts
  * Structured submissions for both sides

* **Authorities**

  * List of Authorities
  * Citations (Bluebook/other formats)

* **Annexures**

  * Relevant extracts from judgments
  * Supporting documents and exhibits

---

## 🧠 Purpose

This repository is designed to:

* Organize legal research systematically
* Facilitate efficient memorial drafting
* Maintain version control of arguments and edits
* Provide quick access to all moot-related materials

---
